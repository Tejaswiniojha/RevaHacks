How Does It Work?

To facilitate live demo of our model, we add two functionalities Register and Verify. Checkout the description below on how to use them.

Register

Stores real signature of a user against a username

This step is performed when a new customer account is created in the bank and a sample of thier real signature is taken.

A single copy of real signature is stored in the database against their username

  • Enter the username of the customer whose signature is to be uploaded
  • Upload the real signature of the customer
  • Press Submit to move store it in DB and move to signature verification

Verify

Takes in a signature and username, matches it with real signature stored in DB

This step is performed when a signature is to be verified against a real signature of a customer identified by their username.

Both the signatures are then passed to the ML model which predicts if it is forged or real

  • Enter the username of the customer whose signature is to be matched
  • Upload the signature to be matched
  • Press Verify to check if it is forged or real
